Student Activities
  • Photographing Club
    The club is organized by students voluntarily. Some lectures on photographing and contest as well as exhibition are held by the club timely. The target of the club is to enrich students’ campus life and let them record memorable things in life with their cameras.


  • Parkour Association
    Parkour Association is established in Dec. 2010. They take the pursuit of freedom as their main target. Parkour is a kind of activity which advocates passionate art. It means you can make exercises anywhere, anytime. The whole city can be viewed as your playground; you even can climb onto the house roof.


  • Jiu Ge Stage Play Club
    Jiu Ge Stage Play Club is established on Nov. 25th, 2009 after the approval of Youth League Committee of Guizhou Normal College. In the principle of creating literary drama and promoting stage art, we put the drama art on the stage to enrich students’ extracurricular life. This club mainly performs musical, cross-talk, witty skits and so on. We have 9 divisions in charge of stage, costumes, make-up, publicity, etc.
